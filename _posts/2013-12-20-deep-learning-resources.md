Here is a collection of resources about deep learning neural networks. I have not read all of the papers or watched all of the videos, so I cannot vouch for them.

## Websites:

* [Deep Learning](http://deeplearning.net/):

  Deep Learning is a new area of Machine Learning research, which has been introduced with the objective of moving Machine Learning closer to one of its original goals: Artificial Intelligence.

  This website is intended to host a variety of resources and pointers to information about Deep Learning. In these pages you will find

  + a [reading list](http://deeplearning.net/?page_id=4),
  + links to [software](http://deeplearning.net/?page_id=12),
  + [datasets](http://deeplearning.net/?page_id=31),
  + a [discussion forum](http://deeplearning.net/forum),
  + as well as [tutorials](http://deeplearning.net/?page_id=7) and cool [demos](http://deeplearning.net/?page_id=29).

  For the latest additions, including papers and software announcement, **be sure to visit the [Blog](http://deeplearning.net/?page_id=13) section** of the website. [Contact us](mailto:admin@deeplearning.net) if you have any comments or suggestions!
* [Geoffrey E. Hinton](http://www.cs.toronto.edu/%7Ehinton/): Papers, including tutorials, and videos.
* [Jürgen Schmidhuber](http://www.idsia.ch/%7Ejuergen/): Papers and links.
