I posted little while before [" Firefox add-ons for your secure internet"](http://erogol.com/2012/08/18/firefox-add-ons-for-your-secure-internet/ "Permalink to Firefox add-ons for your secure internet") and now I found more  add-ons I think you might see useful as the before ones.

[![](https://static-ssl-cdn.addons.mozilla.net/img/uploads/addon_icons/363/363974-64.png?modified=1335486021)](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/collusion/?src=api) **Collision** shows you the web sites that follow your internet movement on a visual graph. It is really interesting to see the ones on a such a graph while seeing the data flow connections between. It is a good tool and [for more information and download...](http://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/collusion/?src=api)

[![](https://www.anonymox.net/files/logo.jpg?nocache)](https://www.anonymox.net/en) changes your identification information on internet by setting your ip address and location information. In that way the one who try to catch your information is be misleaded. However it comes with two customer opportunities. You might be a free user but it causes some noisy advertisements on your browser or might choose to be a premium with a low fee. Anyway, For anonymity on internet anonymox is a great add-on. [For more information and download...](https://www.anonymox.net/en)

[![](http://www.ghostery.com/images/Ghostery-Logo-TM.png)](https://www.ghostery.com/) is another blocker for the websites watching you. It detects who is watching you, gives full identification about the tracker and gives the chance of setting who is allowed, who is not. [For more information and download...](https://www.ghostery.com/)

That's all for now. If you know some tool more please share with me to post later.
