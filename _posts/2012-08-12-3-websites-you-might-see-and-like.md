[![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/uploads.uservoice.com/logo/design_setting/79765/original/logoMusicovery_7324_0.png?1322788258 "musicovery")](http://www.musicovery.com) takes your mood status with a click on a good visual and gives you some music you might like. It works pretty well and in easy manner.

[![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/static.tumblr.com/x8xyekb/w6Wm83m39/logo-full-wht.jpg "frequency logo")](http://www.frequency.com) is collecting online videos from various social media resources like Twitter, Facebook and others. You can collect videos into personalized video channel. If you want to see some collection of videos according to your interests, Frequency is just for you.

[![](https://wpuploads.appadvice.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Pocket_Logo_Small.png "pocket")](https://getpocket.com) is read later application that comes with all the tools work with your smart phone or web browser and makes your information search life easier. In addition with its tagging function you might reach your pages and sources after you read with the searching feature.
