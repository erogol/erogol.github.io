Answer by John Levingster:  
> **In China you can hire a girlfriend/boyfriend that will pose as your future marriage partner to your family during the Chinese new year holidays.**

Many young Chinese people are pressured by their family to find a suitable marriage partner. Especially during Chinese new year, when most people go back to their hometown to meet with their family, young people are often asked "where is your girlfriend/boyfriend/when are you getting married.." etc. Hence, to escape all the stress and pressure, they simple hire someone that poses as their boy/girlfriend and as result they're not bothered much by their family anymore.

Prices are set per day, ranging from 100USD-1000USD. And you can find them online on China's "Ebay". Their online profiles include pictures, information about their weight, skin tone, and their acting skills.
